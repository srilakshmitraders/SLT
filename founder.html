Professional Journey

Mr. M. R. Prasada Raju founded Sri Lakshmi Traders with a vision to build a dependable cement supply business rooted in trust and long-term relationships.

Over the past 25 years, he has successfully expanded the business by consistently meeting customer expectations and maintaining strong partnerships with leading brands.

His deep understanding of market dynamics and customer needs has enabled the company to sustain growth and reliability over decades.

Leadership Philosophy

His leadership is centered on three key principles:

  • Consistency in supply and service
  • Commitment to quality and reliability
  • Strong customer-first approach

These values have shaped Sri Lakshmi Traders into a trusted name in the construction supply ecosystem of Visakhapatnam.

Business Impact

Under his leadership, Sri Lakshmi Traders has achieved recognition as a top-performing dealer in Nagarjuna Cements and established a strong presence across multiple brands.

The company continues to serve a wide customer base, including contractors, builders, and individual clients, maintaining a reputation for reliability and professionalism.
